ADMISSIONS AND FEE STRUCTURE

  • Is open to all boys and girls irrespective of caste or creed.
  • The admission shall be done after an interview. Only those children who is the opinion of the Principal are able to follow the set curriculum will be considered for admission
 

REGISTRATION

  • Registration must be done on the prescribed from attached. Kindly see the school notice board for the dates and the timing if any.
  • Registration of a candidate does not guarantee his/her admission.
  • The interviews and written test will be held on the date assigned to you on the registration form.
  • Fee once deposited will not be refunded.
  • The management has absolute freedom in the choice of candidates for admission in the interest of the institution.
 

NOTE -

  • Fees will be taken in three installements.
    • 1st Installement - At the time of Admission or Month July.
    • 2nd Installement - At the month of October/November after Diwali.
    • 3rd Installement - At the month of January.
  • Late Fees will be charged for delay payment.
  • Charges of books, uniform, stationary and rent of bus will taken separately.
  • We accept fees by cheques, D.D, NEFT, RTGS and direct deposition in our bank accounts.
  • Tuition Fees will be deposited in 2nd and third installements. Other charges will be taken in 1st installements.
  • 10% discount will be given for full payment in month of July.
