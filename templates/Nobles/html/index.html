Shri Abhay Nobles School has been established by a young enthusiastic person, who is highly qualified and well trained and who also knows child psychology. He fully dedicated to education, and imparting is his passion. He thinks to teach English in a specialised manner in guiding the students, motivating them and teach ing English in a specialised way to the student with rural background Recognising the rural talent and leading them to a better future is his life's aim.
